About Us

Light for Community is a community-based initiative that helps marginalized communities access quality education and health services. We focus on rebuilding schools and fighting malnutrition to ensure every child achieves their full potential.

  • Mission: A community where every child has access to quality education and good health, regardless of their background.
  • Vision: Empower communities through education, health, compassion, and sustainable impact.

Adima School Renovation

We are renovating Adima Primary and Secondary School to provide modern classrooms, a reliable water supply, and daily nutrition for children. Follow our progress and see the difference your support makes!
